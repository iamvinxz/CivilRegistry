Overview of the PSA CENOMAR

The PSA CENOMAR (Certificate of No Marriage Record) is an essential document issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority that confirms a person has never been married. This certificate is a key requirement for many important life events such as applying for a marriage license, processing visa applications, and other legal or administrative matters.

Also known as a Certificate of Singleness, the PSA CENOMAR is a vital part of wedding preparations and various official transactions, including employment and loan applications. It serves as official proof of your civil status and is often requested by government agencies and private institutions.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about obtaining your PSA CENOMAR—whether you choose to apply online or prefer to do a walk-in application. You’ll find step-by-step instructions for applying, details on delivery options, accepted payment methods, and tips to ensure your personal information is kept safe throughout the process. With this information, you can confidently and efficiently secure your PSA CENOMAR when you need it most.
