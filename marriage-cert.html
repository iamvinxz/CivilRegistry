Overview of the PSA Marriage Certificate


A PSA Marriage Certificate is an official document issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) that serves as legal proof of a couple’s marriage. It becomes a vital record once your civil status changes to “married,” and is often required for a wide range of legal and personal transactions.

This certificate may be needed when applying for joint bank accounts, processing insurance claims and pension benefits, or renewing your passport through the Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA). It is also used to support visa applications, property transactions, and other government-related processes.

Whether you prefer the ease of online services or wish to apply in person, it is important to understand the proper steps for obtaining your PSA Marriage Certificate. From submitting your request to receiving your copy, this guide outlines both online and walk-in application options, payment procedures, and delivery timelines to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience.
